As we informed earlier, the 2025 military competition took place from April 22 to 26. In it, the winners and prizewinners were determined in ten disciplines of military-applied and Olympic sports, including hand-to-hand combat, army biathlon, knife throwing, stone lifting, badminton, athletics, national wrestling, shooting, and three types of wrestling, both in individual and team categories.

The Ministry of Defense, in cooperation with a number of ministries and agencies, is conducting public mobile receptions and job fairs to socially support military personnel and their family members, as well as to study and solve existing problems by visiting local areas.

A memorandum of cooperation was signed between the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the Northwestern Military District, and the "IT Training Center Ustudy" at the Department of Defense of the Republic of Karakalpakstan. The memorandum aims to organize additional training courses in the field of information technology for the servicemen of the Nukus garrison mobilization call-up reserve battalion.
