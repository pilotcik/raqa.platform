ARMY UZBEKISTAN

The armed forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan include military associations, formations and units established and maintained by the state to protect the national interests, sovereignty, territorial integrity and peaceful life of the population, to repeal and provent wars and armed conflicts. (Ministry of Defence, Ministry of Internal Affairs, Ministry of Emergency Situations, State Security Service and National Guard) The Armed Forces of the Republic of Uzbekistan (Uzbek: O'zbekiston Respublikasi Qurolli Kuchlari, Ўзбекистон Республикаси Қуролли Кучлари), is the name of the unified armed forces of Uzbekistan, consisting of the Ground Force and the Air and Air Defence forces under the defence ministry. Paramilitary units include the National Guard,[2] a Frontier Service[3] and a River Force. It is reported to be the largest and the strongest in Central Asia.[4]
